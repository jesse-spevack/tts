## Detailed Description

**Turn articles into podcast episodes instantly.**

TTS Podcast is the companion extension for Very Normal TTS — a service that converts web articles into high-quality podcast episodes you can listen to anywhere.

### How It Works

1. **Browse** to any article you want to listen to later
2. **Click** the TTS Podcast icon in your toolbar
3. **Listen** to your article as a podcast episode in your favorite podcast app

That's it. No copying URLs, no switching tabs, no friction.

### Features

• **One-Click Conversion** — Click the icon and the article is sent to your podcast feed automatically

• **Smart Extraction** — Intelligently extracts article content, removing ads, navigation, and clutter

• **Works Everywhere** — Compatible with news sites, blogs, Medium, Substack, and most article-based websites

• **Visual Feedback** — Icon badge shows status: loading, success, or error

• **Secure** — Your reading history stays private. We only process articles you explicitly send.

### Requirements

• A Very Normal TTS account (free tier available at podread.app)
• Works with any podcast app that supports custom RSS feeds

### Privacy

This extension:
- Only activates when you click the icon
- Only sends article content you choose to convert
- Does not track your browsing history
- Does not collect personal data beyond what's needed for the service
